What is Whispers?

Whispers is a service that helps you keep up with the work of artists that matter to you. Signing up only takes seconds!

How does it work?

You tell us the names of the artists you like; we notify you whenever they are involved in a new project. We made it as simple as possible.

Why can't I just follow a star's twitter account?

First, not all stars have a twitter account. Second, and more crucially, some celebrities tend to be quite spammy on Twitter. If all you want is a short, timely update when something new comes up, then Twitter is a terribly noisy tool for this.

I want it for music releases / concerts / books / video games / comics / TV shows / everything!

We are considering expanding to these, and more! But it will take some time.

What country are the release dates for?

The United States -- for now. One day, we will be a bit more clever about it, we promise!

Is it free?

Of course, and it will always be.

Where do you get your data?

At the moment, all our movie data comes from themoviedb.org. They do an excellent job, and their data is volunteered and maintained by people like you. If you see something wrong with our data, you can go there and fix it yourself!

IMDb has better data!

Be that as it may, IMDb charge dearly for an application like ours to access to their data. We prefer supporting an open solution, at the expense of the occasional anomaly or missing cast. In our experience, the data is fairly high quality for most films.

What is the philosophy behind Whispers?

We want to save our users time and effort by providing them with the right information at the right time. Our guarantee is twofold: (1) we will never miss a beat: if you follow an artist, you will hear about their movies from us in a timely fashion; and (2) we will only contact you with valuable information. No noise, no unwanted gossips.

Information streams such as those provided by Facebook, Twitter, Google Plus or even RSS are not well suited for our use case.

Why should I use Whispers?

Why not? Sign up for free within seconds and we will strive send you accurate, timely updates on your favourite stars so you're the first to know about their next projects.

When do you send updates?

At the moment, there are three types of updates that trigger a notification:

  1. When it is announced that an artist you follow is involved in a new movie;
  2. When a new trailer is released for a movie that involves an artist you follow;
  3. One week before the US release date of such a movie.

This means that you will typically receive three updates per movie. We plan on offering more flexibility in the future (e.g. so you can opt to get one notification per movie only).

Who is behind the project?

Whispers is developed by Arnaud and Timothee.

Arnaud is a San Francisco-based French entrepreneur.

Timothee is a London-based French economist, data scientist and film lover. Finding his free time increasingly scarce as time goes by, he uses Whispers to keep up with all the artists he fell in love with over the years.

Miriam and Catherine offer invaluable advice and support.
